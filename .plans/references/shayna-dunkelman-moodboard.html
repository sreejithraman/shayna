04

Motion Principles

Breathing
Autonomous, slow pulse. The site is alive independent of user interaction. Subtle opacity shifts, gentle texture movements.
Drifting
Slow, continuous movement. Elements float gently, never static. Creates atmosphere without demanding attention.
Reactive
Responds to user presence. Hover states, cursor proximity, scroll velocity. The site notices without performing.
05

The Threshold Concept

SCROLL TO ENTER
Entry Experience
The Threshold
Full-viewport image of Shayna dissolving into digital waveform textures, viewed through analog grain. The site holds until the user scrolls.

Behavior: Scroll triggers fade and entry into the main content. The user chooses when to cross the boundary.

Feeling: A decompression chamber. Cinematic. The artist appears, then recedes into abstraction.
